Hội An

ANCIENT TOWN

Hoi An is a small town which is situated on the bank of Thu Bon river located in central Vietnam



Hoi An was a bustling international trading port in Southeast Asia from the 15th to the 19th century.


Nowadays, Hoi An is one of the most famous tourist destinations in Vietnam..

Tradition is still very much alive in the Old Town, even though many of the old shops have been converted to modern businesses which aim to foreigners and tourists, Hoi An is considered as a living museum of architecture and urban lifestyle

Most traditional houses here are the combination of different architectures from different cultures such as Chinese, Japanese, French, and British and of course Vietnamese style.

For those who love sea, sun, and sand, Hoi An offers two lovely beaches which are five kilometers away from the town center. Renting a bike, riding around the town to enjoy the peaceful atmosphere is really enjoyable..
